class="mw-headline" id="Accidents_and_incidents">Accidents and incidents</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Orio_al_Serio_International_Airport&amp;action=edit&amp;section=8" title="Edit section: Accidents and incidents">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>\n<ul><li>On 30 October 2005, Trade Air Flight 729 crashed near <a href="/wiki/Bergamo" title="Bergamo">Bergamo</a>, <a href="/wiki/Italy" title="Italy">Italy</a>, shortly after taking off in poor weather. The flight was a night-time cargo flight from Bergamo to <a href="/wiki/Zagreb_International_Airport" class="mw-redirect" title="Zagreb International Airport">Zagreb</a> operated by a <a href="/wiki/Let_L-410_Turbolet" title="Let L-410 Turbolet">Let L-410 Turbolet</a> with the <a href="/wiki/Aircraft_registration" title="Aircraft registration">registration</a> 9A-BTA. All three people on board, two pilots and a passenger, were killed.<sup id="cite_ref-33" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-33">&#91;33&#93;</a></sup></li>\n<li>On 5 August 2016, during the night, <a href="/wiki/Boeing_737" title="Boeing 737">Boeing 737</a>-476 (SF) registered HA-FAX, operated by <a href="/wiki/ASL_Airlines_Hungary" title="ASL Airlines Hungary">ASL Airlines Hungary</a>, <a href="/wiki/ASL_Airlines_Hungary_Flight_7332" title="ASL Airlines Hungary Flight 7332">overshot while landing</a> on runway 28 in Bergamo and came to a stop on a parking lot and on a secondary highway lane that is around the airport, 300 m from the runway end. No one was injured, but some cars were destroyed and the plane sustained substantial damages. The plane was removed from the street the same day. The air traffic remained unvaried without delays.<sup id="cite_ref-34" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-34">&#91;34&#93;</a></sup></li></ul>\n<h2><span class="mw-headline" express coaches.<sup id="cite_ref-35" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-35">&#91;35&#93;</a></sup> There are further connections to/from Bergamo city center, <a href="/wiki/Arezzo" railway station is currently being built at Bergamo airport by 2024, <sup id="cite_ref-36" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-36">&#91;36&#93;</a></sup> the current nearest railway station is <a href="/wiki/Bergamo_railway_station" title="Bergamo railway station">Bergamo railway station</a>, 3.5 miles (5.6&#160;km) away. There is no official shuttle between the airport and the railway station.
