class="mw-parser-output"><p>A <b>designated activity company</b> (<a href="/wiki/Irish_language" title="Irish language">Irish</a>: <i><b>Cuideachta Ghn\xc3\xadomha\xc3\xadochta Ainmnithe</b></i>) or <b>DAC</b> is a form of company in <a href="/wiki/Ireland" title="Ireland">Ireland</a> created by the <a href="/wiki/Companies_Act_2014" title="Companies Act 2014">Companies Act 2014</a>.\n</p><p>Like a <a href="/wiki/Limited_company" title="Limited company">limited company</a>, designated activity companies have limited liability.
