Safety and Security Agency</b>, also known by the acronym <b>AESA</b> (Spanish: <i>Agencia Estatal de Seguridad A\xc3\xa9rea</i>), is the <a href="/wiki/National_aviation_authority" title="National aviation authority">national aviation authority</a> for <a href="/wiki/Spain" title="Spain">Spain</a>.\n</p><p>The agency is based in Madrid, where it occupies offices on the <a href="/wiki/Paseo_de_la_Castellana" title="Paseo de la Castellana">Paseo de la Castellana</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>\n</p>\n<h2><span
