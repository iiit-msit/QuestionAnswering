href="https://www.worldcat.org/search?fq=x0:jrnl&amp;q=n2:1465-6337">1465-6337</a></td></tr></tbody></table>\n<p><i><b>Airliner World</b></i> is an <a href="/wiki/Aviation" title="Aviation">aviation</a> magazine published by <a href="/wiki/Key_Publishing" title="Key Publishing">Key Publishing</a> in <a href="/wiki/Stamford,_Lincolnshire" title="Stamford, Lincolnshire">Stamford, Lincolnshire</a>, England. In the <a href="/wiki/United_States" title="United States">United States</a>, the magazine is distributed from Key Publishing office in <a href="/wiki/Avenel,_New_Jersey" title="Avenel, New Jersey">Avenel, New Jersey</a>.\n</p><p>The first edition of <i>Airliner World</i> was published in May 1999 <sup id="cite_ref-airi_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-airi-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> and is now the UK\xe2\x80\x99s biggest selling monthly civil aviation magazine.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>\n</p><p><i>Airliner World</i> is dedicated to <a href="/wiki/Airline" title="Airline">airlines</a> and <a href="/wiki/Airliner" title="Airliner">airliners</a>. The magazine publishes worldwide <a href="/wiki/Aviation" title="Aviation">aviation</a> news, as well as articles on regional and worldwide airports and airlines. Another airline publication, <i><a href="/wiki/Air_International" title="Air International">Air International</a></i>, does not represent competition, since both magazines are published by Key publishing. <i>Airliner World</i> is part of a group of aviation magazines published by Key, including <i><a href="/wiki/AIR_International" class="mw-redirect" title="AIR International">AIR International</a></i>, <i><a href="/wiki/Air_Forces_Monthly" class="mw-redirect" title="Air Forces Monthly">Air Forces Monthly</a></i>, and sister magazine, <i><a href="/wiki/Airports_of_the_World" title="Airports of the World">Airports of the World</a></i>.<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>\n</p>\n<div id="toc" class="toc" role="navigation" href="/w/index.php?title=Airliner_World&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Contents">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>\n<p>Each monthly issue of the magazine contains a host of feature articles, a list of the latest commercial aircraft deliveries, a report on air safety including a list of incidents and accidents, and twenty pages of headline "News Review", with separate sections, in order, on Europe, <a href="/wiki/Commonwealth_of_Independent_States" title="Commonwealth of Independent States">CIS</a>, Africa/Middle East, Americas, Asia/Pacific, and News Review International. The magazine focuses predominantly on today\'s commercial aviation and often takes a retrospective look at airlines and airports or events. There is often a section for aircraft simulation review, recent books, DVDs and <a href="/wiki/Model_aircraft" title="Model aircraft">model aircraft</a>, a section on <a href="/wiki/Aircraft_spotting" title="Aircraft spotting">aircraft spotting</a>, all heavily illustrated.\n</p>\n<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Special_features">Special features</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Airliner_World&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Special features">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>\n<p>As specials, the magazine often includes aircraft posters, calendars, airline guides, and airport guides. Occasionally, the magazine holds various contests. These contests have included writing captions, designing <a href="/wiki/Aircraft_livery" title="Aircraft livery">aircraft liveries</a>, photography contests. Once in a while, the magazine will issue a supplement called <i>Global Airline Guide</i>, which contains information on all airliners of the world. The guide contains information about the airlines\' fleets and an analysis of the marked. The supplement comes in two parts either in the July, August, or September issue. The various airport guides includes statistics, facts and figures.\n</p>\n<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Editorial_staff">Editorial staff</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span
