title="Republic of Ireland">Ireland</a>. It was a mixture of original and aggregated content, before moving to entirely original content.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> The website was founded in early 2010 and is owned by Distilled Media.<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>\n</p><p>It was edited by Jennifer O\'Connell in 2010\xe2\x80\x932011,<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> and by Susan Daly between 2011<sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> and August 2019, when Sinead O\'Carroll stepped into the role<sup id="cite_ref-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup> with Daly\xe2\x80\x99s promotion to Managing Editor. The publication employs approximately 20 journalists.<sup id="cite_ref-advert_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-advert-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>\n</p>\n<div id="toc" class="toc" id="Content">Content</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=TheJournal.ie&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Content">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>\n<p><i>TheJournal.ie</i> produces 70 original pieces of content per day.<sup id="cite_ref-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup> The website was originally divided into four components: <i>TheJournal.ie</i> itself for Irish and international news and opinion; <i>Fora</i> for business news; <i><a href="/wiki/The42" class="mw-redirect" title="The42">The42</a></i> (formerly <i>TheScore</i>) for sports news; and <i>The Daily Edge</i> for entertainment and gossip.\n</p><p><i>The Daily Edge</i> and <i>Fora</i> are now defunct.\n</p>\n<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="The42.ie">The42.ie</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=TheJournal.ie&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: The42.ie">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>\n<p><i>The42.ie</i> is an Irish app and sports news website, published by Journal Media. It covers soccer, GAA, rugby, athletics, boxing, MMA and other sports.\n</p><p>The website was formerly known as&#160;<i>TheScore.ie</i>&#160;before changing its name to&#160;<i>The42.ie</i>&#160;in 2014.\n</p><p>The sports site is run by Editor, Adrian Russell, with Niall Kelly acting as Deputy Editor. \n</p><p>In April 2019,&#160;<i>The42.ie</i> was named Sports Media Outlet of the Year at the Irish Sport Federation awards.<sup id="cite_ref-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup>\n</p>\n<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="TheDailyEdge.ie">TheDailyEdge.ie</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=TheJournal.ie&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: TheDailyEdge.ie">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>\n<p>As of 29 March 2019, <i>The Daily Edge</i> no longer produces content.<sup id="cite_ref-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup>\n</p>\n<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Fora.ie">Fora.ie</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=TheJournal.ie&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Fora.ie">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>\n<p>On 9 April 2020, Fora announced it was being shut by the parent company it shared with TheJournal.ie due to a decline in advertising revenue prompting the parent to reduce its costs in the wake of the <a href="/wiki/COVID-19_pandemic_in_the_Republic_of_Ireland" title="COVID-19 pandemic in the Republic of Ireland">COVID-19 pandemic in the Republic of Ireland</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-10"
