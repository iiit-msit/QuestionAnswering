B. Pastine International Airport">Ciampino\xe2\x80\x93G. B. Pastine International Airport</a>, in <a href="/wiki/Rome" title="Rome">Rome</a>, <a href="/wiki/Italy" title="Italy">Italy</a>, suffered multiple <a href="/wiki/Bird_strike" title="Bird strike">bird strikes</a> while landing.<sup id="cite_ref-BBC_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-BBC-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> Of the 172 people on board, two crew and eight passengers received hospital treatment for minor injuries.<sup id="cite_ref-avherald1_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-avherald1-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> The 8-month-old <a href="/wiki/Boeing_737_Next_Generation" title="Boeing 737 Next Generation">Boeing 737-8AS</a> jet used for the flight (registered as EI-DYG)<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> had received a massive amount of damage, which led to it being <a href="/wiki/Total_loss" title="Total loss">written off</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-total_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-total-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> This accident represents the fourth hull loss of a Boeing 737-800.<sup id="cite_ref-ASN_5-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-ASN-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>\n</p>\n<div id="toc" class="toc" role="navigation" aria-labelledby="mw-toc-heading"><input type="checkbox" role="button" id="toctogglecheckbox" href="/w/index.php?title=Ryanair_Flight_4102&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Accident">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>\n<p>The jet struck up to 90 <a href="/wiki/Starlings" class="mw-redirect" title="Starlings">starlings</a><sup id="cite_ref-ASN_5-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-ASN-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> on final approach to <a href="/wiki/Rome_Ciampino_Airport" class="mw-redirect" title="Rome Ciampino Airport">Rome Ciampino Airport</a> which damaged the <a href="/wiki/Port_and_starboard" title="Port and starboard">port</a> (left) side landing gear and both engines. The flight attempted to execute a <a href="/wiki/Missed_approach" title="Missed approach">missed approach</a> after one engine was damaged, but the remaining engine ingested birds as well and was damaged during this maneuver. It was reported that the aircraft left runway 15 for a short time before the flight crew brought it back onto the runway.<sup id="cite_ref-avherald1_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-avherald1-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>\n</p><p>The final report of the accident, investigated by the <a href="/wiki/Agenzia_Nazionale_per_la_Sicurezza_del_Volo" title="Agenzia Nazionale per la Sicurezza del Volo">Agenzia Nazionale per la Sicurezza del Volo</a> (National Agency for the Safety of Flights; ANSV) was released on 20 December 2018, more than 10 years after the accident.<sup id="cite_ref-ANSV_6-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-ANSV-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup>\n</p>\n<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Aftermath">Aftermath</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Ryanair_Flight_4102&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Aftermath">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>\n<p>The airport was closed for 36 hours<sup id="cite_ref-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup> and all traffic was diverted to <a href="/wiki/Leonardo_da_Vinci%E2%80%93Fiumicino_Airport" title="Leonardo da Vinci\xe2\x80\x93Fiumicino Airport">Leonardo da Vinci\xe2\x80\x93Fiumicino Airport</a> due to the jet being stranded on the runway after the port side landing gear collapsed.<sup id="cite_ref-BBC_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-BBC-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-avherald1_2-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-avherald1-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>\n</p><p>This accident caused such substantial damage that the aircraft was written off.
