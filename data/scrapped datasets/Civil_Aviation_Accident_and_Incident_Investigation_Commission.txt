href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> (<a href="/wiki/Spanish_language" title="Spanish language">Spanish</a>: <i lang="es"><b>Comisi\xc3\xb3n de Investigaci\xc3\xb3n de Accidentes e Incidentes de Aviaci\xc3\xb3n Civil</b></i>, <b>CIAIAC</b>) is the <a href="/wiki/Spain" title="Spain">Spanish</a> national agency responsible for air accident investigation. It is a division of the <a href="/wiki/Ministry_of_Public_Works_and_Transport_(Spain)" class="mw-redirect" title="Ministry of Public Works and Transport (Spain)">Ministry of Public Works and Transport</a>. The CIAIAC investigates all the accidents and incidents of civil aircraft that take place in Spanish territory.\n</p><p>The CIAIAC also maintains detailed statistics of all the air accidents and incidents in <a href="/wiki/Spain" title="Spain">Spain</a>.\n</p><p>The headquarters of the CIAIAC are in <a href="/wiki/Latina_(Madrid)" title="Latina (Madrid)">Latina</a>, <a href="/wiki/Madrid"
