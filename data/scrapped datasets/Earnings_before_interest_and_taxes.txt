href="/wiki/Revenue" title="Revenue">revenue</a> \xe2\x80\x93 <a href="/wiki/Operating_expense" title="Operating expense">operating expenses</a> (OPEX) = EBIT \xe2\x80\x93 non-operating profit + non-operating expenses<sup id="cite_ref-investopedia_3-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-investopedia-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup></dd></dl>\n<h2><span class="mw-headline" title="Capital structure">capital structure</a> of a firm (e.g., through a <a href="/wiki/Leveraged_buyout" title="Leveraged buyout">leveraged buyout</a>) first evaluates a firm\'s fundamental expenses (e.g. the <a href="/wiki/Cost_of_goods_sold" title="Cost of goods sold">cost of goods sold</a>, selling and administrative expenses) are subtracted from revenues.<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Net_income" title="Net income">Net income</a> is later obtained by subtracting interest and taxes from the before taxes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Earnings_before_interest_and_taxes&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit
