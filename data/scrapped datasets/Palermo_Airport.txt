class="magnify"><a href="/wiki/File:PalermoIntAirport.jpg" class="internal" title="Enlarge"></a></div>Aerial view</div></div></div>\n<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Early_years">Early years</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Falcone_Borsellino_Airport&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Early years">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>\n<p>GESAP S.p.a. is the airport management company of "<i>Falcone Borsellino</i>" Airport in <a href="/wiki/Palermo" title="Palermo">Palermo</a>. It has a fully paid-up share capital of \xe2\x82\xac15,912,332 divided between the Regional <a href="/wiki/Province_of_Palermo" title="Province of Palermo">Province of Palermo</a>, the <a href="/wiki/Comune" title="Comune">Comune</a> of <a href="/wiki/Palermo" title="Palermo">Palermo</a>, the Chamber of Commerce, the <a href="/wiki/Comune" title="Comune">Comune</a> of <a href="/wiki/Cinisi" title="Cinisi">Cinisi</a> and other minor partners.\n</p><p>Established in 1985, until 1994 GESAP operated exclusively as handler and supplier of ground services for Palermo Airport, the management of which is directly assigned by the government and overseen by the District Airport Directorate.\n</p><p>The airport was given the name <i>Falcone Borsellino</i> in memory of the two leading <a href="/wiki/Anti-mafia" class="mw-redirect" title="Anti-mafia">anti-mafia</a> judges <a href="/wiki/Giovanni_Falcone" title="Giovanni Falcone">Giovanni Falcone</a> and <a href="/wiki/Paolo_Borsellino" title="Paolo Borsellino">Paolo Borsellino</a> who were murdered by the <a href="/wiki/Sicilian_Mafia" title="Sicilian Mafia">Sicilian Mafia</a> in 1992. A 1.90-metre (6&#160;ft 3&#160;in) diameter plaque featuring their portraits can be found to the right of one of the main outside entrances to the departure hall, set into a mosaic of Sicily. Created by the Sicilian sculptor <a href="/wiki/Tommaso_Geraci" title="Tommaso Geraci">Tommaso Geraci</a>, it bears the inscription <i>Giovanni Falcone\xe2\x80\x93Paolo Borsellino\xe2\x80\x93Gli Altri\xe2\x80\x93L\'orgoglio della Nuova Sicilia</i> (<i>Giovanni Falcone\xe2\x80\x93Paolo Borsellino\xe2\x80\x93The Others\xe2\x80\x93The Pride of the New Sicily</i>).\n</p><p>In 1994, GESAP was charged with the partial management of the airport through a convention which granted the company a 20-year mandate to run land-side activities (the airport buildings and surrounding areas).\n</p><p>In April 1999, GESAP obtained an anticipated mandate to manage the airport\'s air side activities, and, more specifically, the flight infrastructure (runways, links, taxiways and aprons) as foreseen by art. 17 L. 135/97.\n</p><p>As airport management company, GESAP plans, creates and manages the airport\'s areas, infrastructures and systems, ensuring the necessary maintenance and implementation of the same. It also provides centralised services such as airport coordination, public information systems, security controls and surveillance as well as managing commercial outlets through concessions to third parties.\n</p>\n<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Development_since_the_2000s">Development since the 2000s</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Falcone_Borsellino_Airport&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Development since the 2000s">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>\n<p>In April 2004, GESAP was awarded UNI ISO 9001/2000 (Vision 2000) certification. The company had already received certification for its services and processes in the handling sector and this too was renewed by the certification body, <a href="/wiki/T%C3%9CV" class="mw-redirect" title="T\xc3\x9cV">T\xc3\x9cV</a>, in December 2006.\n</p><p>On 30 May 2004, ENAC awarded GESAP an "airport certificate" in recognition of the airport\'s full conformity with the regulations set down in ENAC\'s "regulations for the construction and management of airports". On 24 May 2007 GESAP has obtained the renewal of the airport certificate until 30 May 2010.\n</p><p>Today, after having recently transferred its handling sector to a controlled company, GH Palermo, GESAP is awaiting a ministerial decree that will grant it a forty-year concession for the total management of the airport. This comes after the deliberation of ENAC\'s board of directors on 1 March 2005 that was officialised in a convention signed on 17 November 2006.\n</p>\n<h2><span class="mw-headline" It links the airport with <a href="/wiki/Palermo_Centrale_railway_station" title="Palermo Centrale railway station">Palermo Centrale railway station</a>.
