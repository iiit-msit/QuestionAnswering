class="external free" href="http://elfaa.com">http://elfaa.com</a></td></tr></tbody></table>\n<p>The <b>European Low Fares Airline Association</b> (<b>ELFAA</b>) was an organization formed in 2004 to represent <a href="/wiki/Low-cost_carrier" title="Low-cost carrier">low-cost carriers</a> and lobby European institutions on their behalf.<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> It ceased operations and disbanded in 2016, as major members joined the newly formed trade group <a href="/wiki/Airlines_for_Europe" title="Airlines for Europe">Airlines for Europe</a>. ELFAA was based in <a href="/wiki/Brussels" title="Brussels">Brussels</a>, Belgium.\n</p>\n<div id="toc" class="toc" role="navigation" aria-labelledby="mw-toc-heading"><input type="checkbox" Airways">British Airways</a> and <a href="/wiki/Iberia_(airline)" title="Iberia (airline)">Iberia</a> and low-cost carriers <a href="/wiki/EasyJet" title="EasyJet">EasyJet</a>, <a <a href="/wiki/Wizz_Air" title="Wizz Air">Wizz Air</a>. BA and Iberia joined in 2015, citing similar views regarding air transport liberalisation and position regarding <a href="/wiki/ME3_carriers" title="ME3 carriers">major Middle East airlines</a>.<sup
