create a general purpose aircraft, suitable for both civilian and military roles with a low operational cost and high reliability.\n</p><p>The first prototype, with the military designation <b>YC-95</b>, was flown on 26 October 1968.<sup id="cite_ref-Pioneer_p164_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Pioneer_p164-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> and two other prototypes were built, known as EMB 100.<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> By 1969 an order was placed for 80 production aircraft, by now known as <b>EMB 110 Bandeirante</b>, for the Brazilian Air Force (FAB) with the newly formed aircraft company Embraer. The Bandeirante received its Brazilian <a href="/wiki/Type_certificate" title="Type certificate">airworthiness certificate</a> at the end of 1972.<sup id="cite_ref-Pioneer_p165_5-0"
