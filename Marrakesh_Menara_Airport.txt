section: Terminal">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>\n<p>Menara Airport has two passenger terminals housed in one large building. A third terminal has been built. The existing T1/T2 offer a space of 42,000 m<sup>2</sup> and have a designed capacity of 9 million passengers/year. The separate freight-terminal has 340m<sup>2</sup> covered space<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> The air terminals (1 and 2) are 22,000&#160;m<sup>2</sup> (236,806&#160;sq&#160;ft) and designed to handle 2,500,000 passengers per year. Menara is one of the six airports in Morocco where ONDA offers its special VIP service <a href="/wiki/Moroccan_Airports_Authority#Salon_Convives_de_Marque" title="Moroccan Airports Authority">Salon Convives de Marque</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>\n</p>\n<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Runway_and_apron">Runway and apron</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Marrakesh_Menara_Airport&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Runway and apron">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>\n<p>Aircraft parking space of 125,000 square metres (1,345,489&#160;sq&#160;ft) supports up to fourteen <a href="/wiki/Boeing_737" title="Boeing 737">Boeing 737s</a> and four <a href="/wiki/Boeing_747" title="Boeing 747">Boeing 747s</a>. The cargo terminal is 340&#160;m<sup>2</sup> (3,660&#160;sq&#160;ft) of covered space.<sup id="cite_ref-ONDA-Marrakech_1-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-ONDA-Marrakech-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> The paved <a href="/wiki/Runway" title="Runway">runway</a> is laid out in the direction 10/28 is 3,100 by 45 metres (10,171&#160;ft \xc3\x97&#160;148&#160;ft). It can receive all modern jetliners up to the <a href="/wiki/Boeing_747" title="Boeing 747">Boeing 747</a> in size. The airport is equipped with an <a href="/wiki/Instrument_landing_system#ILS_categories" title="Instrument landing system">ILS Cat II</a> landing system and offers the following radio navigation aids:
