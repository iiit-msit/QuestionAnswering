href="/w/index.php?title=Treviso_Airport&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Overview">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>\n<p>The airport stands 18 meters above sea level.  The runway direction is 07/25 with an asphalt surface 2,420&#160;m (7,940&#160;ft) long and 45&#160;m (150&#160;ft) wide. The new terminal was opened in 2007. It was named after <a href="/wiki/Antonio_Canova" title="Antonio Canova">Antonio Canova</a>, a famous Italian <a href="/wiki/Sculptor" class="mw-redirect" title="Sculptor">sculptor</a>.\n</p><p>In areas. Three are long-term car parks with a total of 564 places. In front of the terminal building there are an additional 50 parking spaces for short term parking.\n</p>\n<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Bus">Bus</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Treviso_Airport&amp;action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Bus">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>\n<p>A public bus service, operated by <a href="/wiki/Mobilit%C3%A0_di_Marca" title="Mobilit\xc3\xa0 di Marca">Mobilit\xc3\xa0 di Marca</a>, connects the airport with the railway station (the <a href="/wiki/Treviso_Centrale_railway_station" title="Treviso Centrale railway station">Treviso Centrale railway station</a>) in the centre of <a href="/wiki/Treviso" title="Treviso">Treviso</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-13" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-13">&#91;13&#93;</a></sup> Another bus service, connecting with flights for <a href="/wiki/Wizzair" class="mw-redirect" title="Wizzair">Wizzair</a> and <a href="/wiki/Ryanair" title="Ryanair">Ryanair</a>, operated by BARZI BUS SERVICE,<sup id="cite_ref-en.barziservice.com_14-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-en.barziservice.com-14">&#91;14&#93;</a></sup> reaches Venice in 40 minutes by highway. Further coach connections are available from the airport or from the city center of Treviso. Daily coach service operated by DRD to <a href="/wiki/Ljubljana" title="Ljubljana">Ljubljana</a> (<a href="/wiki/Slovenia" title="Slovenia">Slovenia</a>) via  <a href="/wiki/Venice_Marco_Polo_Airport" Venezia Giulia Airport">Friuli Venezia Giulia Airport</a> (<a href="/wiki/Trieste" title="Trieste">Trieste</a>)<sup id="cite_ref-15" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-15">&#91;15&#93;</a></sup> A public bus vice versa service from Treviso to <a href="/wiki/Padua" title="Padua">Padua</a>, route 101, is operated by <a href="/wiki/Mobilit%C3%A0_di_Marca" title="Mobilit\xc3\xa0 di
